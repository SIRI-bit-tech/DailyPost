Frequently Asked Questions

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 1-2 business days. For urgent matters, please call the appropriate department directly. Our customer service team is available by phone Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM Eastern Time.

We take accuracy very seriously. To report a factual error, please email our editorial team at corrections@dailyrecordpost.com with "Correction Request" in the subject line. Include the article title, the specific error, and any supporting information that will help us verify the correct information. You can also use our general contact form and select "Request a Correction" as the inquiry type.

Letters to the editor should be sent to letters@dailyrecordpost.com. Please include your full name, address, and daytime phone number for verification purposes (only your name and city will be published). Letters should be 250 words or fewer. We also have a dedicated page for opinion submissions with more detailed guidelines. Visit our Opinion Submission page for more information.

For subscription-related issues, please contact our customer service team at subscriptions@dailyrecordpost.com or call (555) 123-4569. Our team can assist with account access, billing questions, delivery issues, and subscription changes. You can also access your account settings by logging in to our website.

To request permission to republish or license our content, please contact licensing@dailyrecordpost.com with details about the content you wish to use and your intended purpose. Please note that all content is copyrighted, and unauthorized reproduction is prohibited. We generally respond to licensing requests within 3-5 business days.
