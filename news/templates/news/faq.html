Account & Registration

To create an account on The Daily Record Post:

  1. Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of any page
  2. Enter your email address, create a password, and provide your name
  3. Check your email for a verification link
  4. Click the link to verify your account

Once verified, you can log in and access your account dashboard, manage your profile, and engage with our content.

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Click "Log In" in the top right corner
  2. Select "Forgot password?" below the login form
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for password reset instructions
  5. Follow the link to create a new password

If you don't receive the password reset email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact our support team for assistance.

To update your profile information:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Click on your profile picture or name in the top right corner
  3. Select "Profile Settings" from the dropdown menu
  4. Edit your personal information, profile picture, or notification preferences
  5. Click "Save Changes" when you're done

You can update your name, email address, profile picture, and notification preferences at any time.

Content & Editorial

The Daily Record Post publishes new content throughout the day, seven days a week. Our editorial team works around the clock to ensure you have access to the latest news and analysis as events unfold.

Feature articles and in-depth analysis pieces are typically published during weekday mornings, while breaking news is posted as it happens. We update our homepage several times daily to reflect the most important stories.

We take accuracy very seriously and appreciate your help in maintaining our journalistic standards. To report an error:

  1. Scroll to the bottom of the article in question
  2. Click the "Report a Correction" link
  3. Fill out the form detailing the error
  4. Submit your correction request

Alternatively, you can email corrections directly to corrections@dailyrecordpost.com with the article title and a description of the error.

Our editorial team reviews all correction requests promptly, and we will update the article with a correction notice if warranted.

We welcome news tips and story ideas from our readers. You can submit them in several ways:

  • Use our Submit News form for detailed submissions
  • Email tips directly to tips@dailyrecordpost.com
  • For sensitive information requiring confidentiality, you can use our secure tip line: (555) 123-4567

Our editorial team reviews all submissions, though we cannot guarantee that every tip will result in a published story. If we pursue your story idea, a reporter may contact you for additional information.

Technical Support

To clear your reading history on The Daily Record Post:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Click your profile picture or name in the top right corner
  3. Select "Reading History" from the dropdown menu
  4. Click the "Clear All History" button at the top of the page
  5. Confirm your choice when prompted

You can also remove individual articles from your history by clicking the "X" next to each item. Your reading history is private and used to provide you with personalized recommendations and to resume reading where you left off.

If you're experiencing errors when trying to access articles, try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Check your internet connection - Make sure you have a stable connection
  • Clear your browser cache - Old cached data can sometimes cause conflicts
  • Try a different browser - This can help identify if the issue is browser-specific
  • Disable browser extensions - Some ad blockers or privacy extensions can interfere with our site
  • Log out and log back in - This refreshes your session credentials

If you're still experiencing issues, please contact our technical support team at support@dailyrecordpost.com with details about the error, your device, browser, and steps to reproduce the problem.

Billing & Payments

We accept the following payment methods for subscriptions and purchases:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover
  • Digital Wallets: Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal
  • Bank Transfers: ACH payments (for annual subscriptions only)

All transactions are processed securely through our payment provider, and we do not store your complete payment information on our servers. For enterprise or institutional subscriptions, we also accept purchase orders.

To update your payment information:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to Account Settings
  3. Select "Billing Information"
  4. Click "Edit Payment Method"
  5. Enter your new payment details
  6. Click "Save Changes"

Your updated payment method will be used for future billing cycles. If your current payment method is expiring or has been declined, we'll send you an email notification prompting you to update your information.

Receipts are automatically emailed to you each time your subscription renews. To access past receipts:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to Account Settings
  3. Select "Billing History"
  4. Find the transaction you need
  5. Click "View Receipt" or "Download PDF"

For tax or reimbursement purposes, you can also request a formal invoice by contacting our billing department at billing@dailyrecordpost.com with your account information and the specific time period you need documentation for.
