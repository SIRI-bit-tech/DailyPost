Our team reviews all submissions, and while we can't guarantee coverage, we appreciate your contributions to our news reporting.

What we're looking for:

  • Local business news and developments
  • Human interest stories
  • Issues affecting the community
  • Tips on breaking news

Submission Guidelines

What to Include

  • Accurate and complete information
  • Relevant details and context
  • Contact information for verification
  • Photos or supporting documents when available
  • Clear indication of time-sensitive material

What to Avoid

  • Unverified rumors or speculation
  • False or misleading information
  • Content that violates privacy
  • Defamatory or harmful accusations
  • Promotional content disguised as news

What Happens Next?

After submission, our editorial team will review your news tip. If we decide to pursue the story, a reporter may contact you for additional information. Please note that due to the volume of submissions we receive, we cannot guarantee a response to every submission.
