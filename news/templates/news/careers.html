Diversity & Inclusion

At The Daily Record Post, we believe that diversity in our newsroom leads to better journalism. We're committed to building a team that reflects the communities we serve.

We actively work to create an inclusive environment where all employees feel valued, respected, and empowered to do their best work. We recognize that diverse perspectives enrich our storytelling and strengthen our connection with our audience.

Our commitment to diversity includes:

  • Inclusive hiring practices
  • Mentorship programs for journalists from underrepresented backgrounds
  • Regular diversity and inclusion training
  • Employee resource groups
  • Partnerships with organizations that promote diversity in journalism

We welcome applicants of all backgrounds, and do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, national origin, age, disability, or any other protected characteristic.

Our Application Process

1. Application

Submit your resume, cover letter, and any required work samples through our online portal.

2. Initial Screening

Qualified candidates will be contacted for a brief phone interview with our recruiting team.

3. Team Interviews

Selected candidates will meet with the hiring manager and potential teammates.

4. Offer

Successful candidates will receive a formal offer with details about compensation and benefits.
